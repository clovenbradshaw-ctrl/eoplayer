The engine that reads a melody is a predictor: at every note it guesses the next one. Run that forward and it writes. Play a few notes on the keyboard, or load a file and cut it off — it reads what's there (key, register, tempo) and continues onto the same stream. Play three notes and it plays the fourth. The seam (where you stop and it takes over) is marked in the reading and inaudible in playback.
